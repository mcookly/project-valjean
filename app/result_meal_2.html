Nutrient Disclaimers:
\nNotre Dame Campus Dining makes every effort to correctly identify and report all food ingredients, but cannot guarantee the accuracy of nutrition information. The nutritional composition may vary due to processing variables, changes in product formulations, manufacturer\u2019s data, portion size, and cooking and preparation techniques. The information provided may therefore contain some degree of variability and should be considered an approximation.

\n \n

Allergen and Ingredient Disclaimers:
\nNotre Dame Campus Dining makes every effort to correctly identify allergens and ingredients. However, there is always a risk of contamination. Formulation changes or ingredient substitutions can occur without prior notice from the manufacturer. In addition, all foods are prepared and served in kitchens where peanuts, tree nuts, fish, shellfish, wheat, soy, milk, and eggs are present. Students, faculty/staff, and other guests need to understand that the University does not assume any liability for adverse reactions to food that is consumed or served at any Campus Dining location. Students, faculty/staff, and guests with food allergies or other special diet considerations should consult the Campus Dining Dietitian, Jocie Antonelli at jantonel@nd.edu or (574) 631-0106.
